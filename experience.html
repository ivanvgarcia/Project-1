Teaching in Tokyo

After teaching in China, I took a flight to Japan and began teaching at a small start-up. At this company, I learned the various facets of running a business in Tokyo and helped get them off the ground by developing their WordPress blog, helping with marketing materials and streamlining their recruiting process.
